Fake Diamond Set & Tiny Plastic Babies

What is this pack for? This pack Fake Diamond Set & Tiny Plastic Babies is one of my more complex packs adding custom armour and (not so) tiny plastic babies! unlike the other packs this requires some effort!.

How is it more complex?

The first thing you'll need is the anvil yk three iron blocks and 4 iron ingots and some exp.
Once you have that you'll need netherite armour or snowballs the armour will become fake diamonds the snowballs will become babies weird I know but trust me, heres a visual tutorial for both the babies and the armour but the long and short of it is you name the items in an anvil its not case sensitive so knock yourselves out
